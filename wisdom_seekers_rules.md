# Wisdom Seekers Game: Official Rule Book

## Overview
Welcome to **Wisdom Seekers**, a fun and insightful card game inspired by the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita. Players alternate as Seekers presenting life's challenges to Wise Gurus, who respond using wisdom cards. The wisest Guru earns the Seeker’s challenge as a reward. Collect the most challenge cards to become the ultimate Wisdom Seeker!

---

## Components
- **106 Cards Total:**
  - **70 Wisdom Cards:** Containing quotes and teachings from the Bhagavad Gita.
  - **36 Scenario Cards:** Challenges divided into 4 categories:
    - **Personal Dilemmas** *(Blue)*
    - **Professional Challenges** *(Green)*
    - **Moral/Ethical Decisions** *(Red)*
    - **Relationship Situations** *(Yellow)*

*Use category colors to include or exclude scenarios as desired.*

---

## Game Setup
- Shuffle Wisdom and Scenario decks separately. Deal **5 Wisdom Cards** to each player.
- Select the Scenario categories you wish to play with. Shuffle and form one Scenario deck.
- Decide the first Seeker. The remaining players are Wise Gurus.

---

## Gameplay
Each round consists of the following steps:

### Step 1: Present the Scenario
The Seeker draws a Scenario card, reads it aloud, and says:
> **"Oh wise gurus, I have come to you with my problem, please give me a solution to it."**

### Step 2: Wise Gurus Respond
- Each Guru selects **one Wisdom card** from their hand and places it face down in front of them.
- Once all Gurus have selected their cards, each Guru reveals their Wisdom card and briefly explains how it best solves the Seeker’s scenario.
- Used Wisdom cards are placed into a discard pile. If the main Wisdom deck runs out, reshuffle the discard pile to create a new Wisdom deck.

### Step 3: Choosing the Wisest Guru
- The Seeker evaluates each response and awards the Scenario card to the Guru whose wisdom they find most insightful and relevant.

### Step 4: Refill Wisdom Cards
- Gurus refill their hand to **5 Wisdom Cards**.
- The next player clockwise becomes the new Seeker, and a new round begins.

---

## Winning the Game
- Continue playing until all Scenario cards have been resolved or until a pre-agreed number of rounds.
- The player with the most Scenario cards collected wins and is declared the ultimate **Wisdom Seeker**.

---

## Optional Variations
- **Anonymous Wisdom:** Gurus submit Wisdom cards anonymously for unbiased evaluation.
- **Speed Wisdom:** Gurus have 30 seconds to present wisdom, emphasizing intuition.
- **Selective Categories:** Customize your gameplay by selecting specific Scenario card categories.

Enjoy your journey towards wisdom and may the wisest Guru win!
